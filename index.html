New Families & Program Overview

Welcome to Andover Mavericks Ultimate! We are a high school Ultimate Frisbee program competing in the Minnesota Ultimate High School Spring League. This section provides new families with a clear overview of how the season works and what to expect.

Season at a Glance

  • Season runs: TBD (typically early March – early June)
  • Official practices begin: TBD
  • Games begin: TBD
  • League: Minnesota Ultimate (MNU) High School Spring League
  • Home practice field: Heritage Oaks Park, Ham Lake

Weekly rhythm includes practices on Mondays and Fridays, with games on Thursday evenings for Open teams. We also participate in local tournaments, team events, and fundraisers throughout the season.

Teams

  • Open Varsity
  • Open JV
  • Girls’ Team (dependent on registration numbers; if numbers are low, girls play on the Open team)

Varsity Tryouts

Varsity tryouts are held during regular practice times (TBD dates). Players not trying out still attend practice. Rosters are announced before the first week of games.

Game Schedule & Game-Day Protocol

  • Games start: TBD
  • Open team games: Thursdays at 6:00 PM (arrival at 5:00 PM)
  • Girls’ team schedule: Varies depending on league placement (TBD)

For Players:

  • Arrive 1 hour before game time
  • Bring dark jersey, light jersey, cleats, and water
  • Practice like you play

For Parents:

  • Cheer positively
  • Avoid discussing playing time during or immediately after games
  • Avoid on-field calls or disputes
  • Model “Spirit of the Game”

Registration – Two Required Steps

  1. Mavericks Registration: Complete the Mavericks registration form, submit down-payment or full season fee, and complete the season logistics survey (TBD deadlines).
  2. Minnesota Ultimate (MNU) Registration – $60: Register using the link provided by the Mavericks and complete all waivers before attending gameplay practices.

What Do Team Fees Cover?

  • MNU team registration
  • Tournament bid fees
  • Equipment (discs, cones, pinnies, first-aid kits)
  • Player jersey
  • Coaching stipends
  • Indoor facility rentals
  • End-of-season celebration

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We want every interested athlete to be able to play. For Mavericks fee assistance, email andovermavericks@gmail.com. Minnesota Ultimate also offers financial aid for league memberships.
